About SOYJAK

$Soyjak- A portmanteau of "soy" and "wojak

Welcome to Soyjak a community-driven meme project celebrating one of the internets most recognizable reaction faces.

Here, we explore the humor, culture, and creativity behind the Soyjak meme, from its origins in online forums to the countless variations created by artists and shitposters around the world.

Whether youre here to laugh, contribute, or simply learn, Soyjak offers a light-hearted look at one of the webs most iconic characters.

This project was created to document, explore, and celebrate the characters journey from a simple reaction face to a sprawling ecosystem of variations, art styles, communities, and inside jokes. Over the years, Soyjak has transformed into a symbol of satire, parody, and commentary, reflecting the ever-changing nature of online expression.

How To Buy

1) Create a Wallet

Download the MetaMask extension from the official website and install it in your browser. Fund it by transferring ETH from an exchange or another wallet.

2) Connect to DEX

Connect to a decentralized exchange (DEX) like Uniswap, SushiSwap, or 1inch. Open the DEX website and click on "Connect Wallet". Then select MetaMask.

3) Swap ETH for SOYJAK

To swap BASE ETH for SOYJAK, select ETH in the "from" field and choose SOYJAK in the "to" field using our contract address. Enter the amount of tokens you want & press swap.

4) Verify & manage tokens

Verify your transaction status in MetaMask under the "Activity" tab or on Basescan using your Ethereum address.
